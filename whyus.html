Choices

At Sankalpa, we believe in a conscious lifestyle where elegance meets ethical living. Crafted in-house using local techniques, our garments from production to purchase embody eco-conscious choices. In a rising ethical fashion market, our bespoke designs and circularity commitment redefine fashion volume. Join us in contributing to a thriving sustainable market, where every choice makes a positive impact. With transparency and environmental responsibility at our core, your wardrobe choices transcend fashion — they embrace a sustainable future.

TRUST

Search Data

Despite lingering skepticism, particularly among previous generations favoring "authentic" and "new" items, data from Google Analytics paints a different picture. A comparison of searches for "new fashion brands" and "new sustainable fashion brands" reveals a notable positive increase, signifying a growing interest in sustainability within the fashion industry. While not surpassing the search frequency of "new brand," the comparable data underscores the undeniable emergence and significance of sustainability.

Certifications

In recent years, the issue of ethical textile production has evolved through distinct phases. Brands now seek global certifications and standards to combat greenwashing and authenticate their ethical practices. Sankalpa stands out with three such certifications: the Positive Luxury Butterfly Tag, the Forest Stewardship Council Certification, and the OKEOTEX MADE IN GREEN Tag. These certifications collectively affirm Sankalpa's commitment to ethical and sustainable practices in the textile industry.
