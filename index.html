Background

Who We Are

Sankalpa is a bespoke brand that produces designer pieces using natural fabrics, hand-loomed by artisans in Kathmandu.

Natural Fabrics-Commitment

Dedicated to an eco-living, we produce exclusively using natural fabrics, hand spun with love that you can trace.

Bespoke Mindset

Each garment is created for the wearer, ensuring durability and comittment through our "ReClaim" policy .

Circularity & Transparency

Our "GarmentTag" feature provides the details of each garment and the love that was put into this craft.

BELIEVE

Focusing On What Matters Most

Sustainability. Community. Quality.

Sankalpa has been developed with all aspects of our footprint in mind. From the fabric production process, which complies with the Local empowerment Act till the collection and Re-Claim policy that allows our customers to mend their garments and choose from three different options to make their garments last, Our Close-looped model is one that has been years in the making.

TRUST

Famili-arity Program

Committed to be the alternative to this fast fashion lifestyle, Our Famili-arity program is designed exclusively for our Famili-s; individuals who believe in our values, who believe in Sankalpa (संकल्प). This program includes an intimate bond with our team and work, and the priority for our Saafa collection (made with fabric-scraps from the production process) that is released each spring.
